Public Event

Our public events, held across the world, celebrate and share achievements awarded the
Nobel Prize in science, literature and peace.

We want to create contexts that inspire people to think, act and challenge each other, and
encourage cross disciplinary dialogue in issues that are crucial to our
world and our future.
